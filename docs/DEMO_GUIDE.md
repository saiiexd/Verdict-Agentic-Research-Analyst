# Demo Guide

Procedures for demonstrating Verdict during interviews, portfolio presentations, or product pitches.

---

## 1. Optimal Demo Sequence

1. **Dashboard Overview**:
   - Begin at the `/dashboard` viewport. Show the aggregated KPI cards, your watchlist tracker, recent research runs, and customization options.
2. **Execute Research Run**:
   - Navigate to the Research view, enter a stock symbol (e.g. `AAPL`), and launch the analysis.
   - Highlight the live agent execution timeline as it updates from financial scraping to critic debate.
3. **Structured Report Experience**:
   - Show the final completed report. Highlight the rating score, financial metrics cards, and news sentiment distribution charts.
   - Toggle **Focus Mode** to show the distraction-free reading layout.
4. **Command Palette**:
   - Press `Ctrl+K` to open the Command Palette. Show how you can navigate pages, toggle theme states, and search previous reports instantly.
